Salad Recipe

Description

Salad is raw greens (such as lettuce) often combined with other vegetables and toppings and served especially with dressing.

Ingredients

  • Shallot
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Jarred pepperoncini
  • Green olives
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Chopped the ingredients.
  2. Make the salad dressing.
  3. Mix the chopped salad with the dressing.
